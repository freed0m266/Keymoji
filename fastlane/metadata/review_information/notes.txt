Keymoji requests "Allow Full Access" solely to use the haptic feedback and key click sound APIs, which iOS gates behind Full Access for keyboard extensions. The app contains no analytics and collects no data. Full Access is optional — all typing features work without it.

No demo account is needed: the app has no login, accounts, or server. Just add the Keymoji keyboard in Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Add New Keyboard, then optionally enable Allow Full Access to hear/feel key clicks.

Promotional Plus offers (please note): The app includes a promotional easter-egg — typing "hesoyam" on the keyboard grants a one-time 60-day Keymoji Plus bonus that stacks onto the user's existing Plus trial. To test: install, type hesoyam in any text field, and observe the unlock. The app also offers an explicit opt-in 30-day Plus trial in onboarding and in Settings — both gifts together can extend Plus to ~90 days, and the user pays zero. Neither bypasses in-app purchase: Keymoji Plus is also available via the standard non-consumable IAP, and no payment is taken outside Apple.
