## Description (max 4000)

```
Keymoji is the iPhone keyboard for people who live in emoji. It puts your favorite emoji one tap away — in the order YOU choose — while keeping the fast, familiar feel of the native keyboard you already know.

Think of it as the best of both worlds: the precision and native feel of Apple's keyboard, plus the power you'd expect from SwiftKey — without the bloat or the tracking.

EMOJI, YOUR WAY

• Pick your favorite emoji and arrange them in your own order
• An emoji row sits right above the keys — your top emoji, always one tap away
• Search any emoji by name — just type what you mean
• Slack-style shortcodes — type :smile: and get the emoji
• The full single-character emoji catalog, built in

A KEYBOARD THAT FEELS RIGHT

• Native look and feel — designed to match iOS, no jarring redesign
• Always-on number row — digits without switching layouts
• QWERTY and QWERTZ, switchable in a tap
• Smart word suggestions that learn the words you actually use
• Long-press any key for accents and diacritics
• Trackpad cursor — long-press the space bar to move the cursor precisely
• Delete word-by-word on a long hold
• Haptic feedback and key click sounds, fully adjustable
• Light/Dark appearance override, independent of the system

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT

Keymoji is also refreshingly private — not as a gimmick, but because it's simple. It makes no network requests at all: there's no networking code in the app, so what you type never leaves your iPhone. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no third-party SDKs. The words it learns to speed up typing stay in a private container that only Keymoji can read — never uploaded, not even to Apple. The source code is public on GitHub.

ABOUT "ALLOW FULL ACCESS"

iOS requires custom keyboards to enable "Allow Full Access" before they can use the haptic feedback and key click sound APIs. That's an iOS sandbox rule, not a data-collection switch. Keymoji uses Full Access only for:

• Haptic feedback (vibration) when you type
• Key click sounds

Keymoji never uses Full Access for networking, your contacts, your location, or anything else — it contains no networking code at all. If you don't want haptics or sound, you can leave Full Access off and the rest of the keyboard works exactly the same.

iPhone only. English keyboard.
```
