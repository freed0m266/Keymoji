## Description (max 4000)

```
Keybo is a custom iPhone keyboard built on one principle: what you type is nobody's business but yours.

Most keyboards phone home. Keybo doesn't. It makes no network requests at all — there's no networking code in the app, so your words never leave your device. No analytics, no crash reporting, no third-party SDKs, nothing to opt out of.

WHY KEYBO

• 100% on-device — Keybo makes no network requests by design. We never receive, transmit, or store anything you type.
• No tracking, ever — no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising SDKs.
• Fast and native-feeling — designed to match the iOS keyboard you already know.
• Open and verifiable — the source code is public on GitHub.

FEATURES

• QWERTY and QWERTZ layouts, switchable in a tap
• Emoji your way — quick emoji key, favorites, search, and the full single-character emoji catalog
• Slack-style shortcodes — type :smile: to get an emoji
• Smart word suggestions that learn the words you use — all stored privately on your device, never uploaded
• Haptic feedback and key click sounds, fully adjustable
• Light/Dark appearance override, independent of the system
• Long-press any key for accents and diacritics
• Trackpad cursor — long-press the space bar to move the cursor precisely
• Delete word-by-word on a long hold

ABOUT "ALLOW FULL ACCESS"

iOS requires custom keyboards to enable "Allow Full Access" before they can use the haptic feedback and key click sound APIs. That's an iOS sandbox rule, not a data-collection switch. Keybo uses Full Access only for:

• Haptic feedback (vibration) when you type
• Key click sounds

Keybo never uses Full Access for networking, your contacts, your location, or anything else — it contains no networking code at all. If you don't want haptics or sound, you can leave Full Access off and the rest of the keyboard works exactly the same.

PRIVACY

Keybo collects no data. None. There's no account, no sign-in, and no server. To make typing faster, Keybo remembers some words you type, but they're stored in a private container on your iPhone that only Keybo can read — never uploaded, not even to Apple. You can clear learned words anytime in Settings, and turn suggestions off entirely.

iPhone only. English keyboard.
```
